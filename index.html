About Casa da Mininha

Discover the perfect blend of traditional Portuguese charm and modern comfort

Your Home Away From Home

Casa da Mininha is a lovingly restored traditional Portuguese home that captures the essence of authentic Minho region living. Our house features original stone walls, wooden beams, and traditional azulejo tiles, all thoughtfully combined with modern amenities for your comfort.

Located in the historic center of Ponte da Barca, you'll be immersed in centuries of Portuguese culture while enjoying easy access to the natural beauty of the Lima Valley and the nearby Peneda-Gerês National Park.

Perfect Location in Ponte da Barca

Explore the beauty and culture of northern Portugal

Historic Center Location

Casa da Mininha is perfectly situated in the heart of Ponte da Barca's historic center. This charming medieval town sits along the Lima River and serves as a gateway to the stunning Peneda-Gerês National Park.

What's Nearby

  • Historic Bridge: 2-minute walk to the iconic medieval bridge
  • Lima River: Beautiful riverside walks and swimming spots
  • Local Restaurants: Traditional Portuguese cuisine within walking distance
  • Peneda-Gerês National Park: 15-minute drive to hiking trails and waterfalls
  • Viana do Castelo: 45-minute drive to the coast and beaches
  • Braga: 1-hour drive to historic city and Bom Jesus sanctuary
